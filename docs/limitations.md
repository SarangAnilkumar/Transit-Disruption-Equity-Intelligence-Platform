# Limitations

- GTFS-Realtime snapshots are observations at capture times, not complete official reliability records.
- Feed availability, latency, and authentication requirements can change without notice.
- Stop-to-SA2 mapping requires geospatial preprocessing and introduces spatial join assumptions.
- SA2 statistical boundaries are not equivalent to real passenger catchments.
- Route-to-SA2 coverage is a network exposure proxy and should not be interpreted as ridership demand.
- Disruption exposure is a derived analytical proxy, not a direct service quality ground truth.
- MVP coverage is metro trains only; buses and trams are deferred to later phases.
- Text quality and field completeness in service alerts can vary significantly over time.
